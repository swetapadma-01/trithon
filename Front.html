Common Ingredients that are prone to climate change

Peanut

You can replace or substitute seeds and seed butter with many peanuts options!.

Treenuts

You can replace or substitute seeds and seed butter with many treenuts options!.

Dairy Milk

Most milk substitutes are a simple one-to-one ratio, and we have quick and easy recipes to help you out!

Egg

There are many store-bought egg substitute options, along with recipes for baking substitutes and recipes for replacing egg whites in meringues, foams, or soups!

Wheat

There are many store-bought wheat substitute options, but make sure to read your labels!

Shellfish

You can always replace shellfish with other lean proteins or mushrooms (such as oyster or shitake mushrooms).

Soyabean

You can always replace soyabean with Coconut aminos.

SeaFood

You can replace seafood by oysters , algae, wildfish and sea weed .
