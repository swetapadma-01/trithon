You might think that shellfish are relatively easy to avoid. However, many condiments and prepared foods contain shellfish-based ingredients. Don't assume that "imitation crab," sometimes called surimi, is shellfish-free. Instead, look at the product's ingredients to determine whether the manufacturer uses shellfish in its recipe. You should also carefully check the ingredient list on fish sauces. Kosher foods should not contain shellfish. You can always replace shellfish with other lean proteins or mushrooms (such as oyster or shitake mushrooms).
